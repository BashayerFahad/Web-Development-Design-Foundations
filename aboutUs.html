About Us


Welcome to Chocolics Hideway, where indulging in your chocolate dreams is only a click away! We're passionate about bringing the finest chocolates from around the world to our fellow chocolate lovers in Saudi Arabia. Whether you're craving a creamy ganache, a vibrant fruit truffle, or a dark, decadent bar, Chocolics has something to tantalize your taste buds.


Our Story

Chocolics began with a simple mission: to share our love for the world's most beloved treat. We felt KSA deserved a source of premium, diverse chocolates, curated with care and delivered with delight. So, we set out to build a haven for chocolate connoisseurs and casual enthusiasts alike.




What makes us special

Gifting Made Easy: Our beautiful packaging and thoughtful arrangements make Chocolics the perfect choice for any occasion, from gardauation to weddings to just-because moments.

Delivering Delight: We understand the importance of a smooth and swift journey for your chocolates. We offer secure, nationwide delivery to ensure your treats arrive fresh and ready to savor.
